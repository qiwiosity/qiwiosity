Design RationaleHow this becomes the whole site

The whole product gets a stronger promise.

Qiwiosity should not just answer "what is nearby?" It should answer "what kind of New Zealand day should I have?" That shift changes the visual language, the information hierarchy, and the business model.

HomepageLead with immersive image, emotional trip modes, and one search deck instead of dense navigation.
BrowseGroup places by visitor intent: wild, calm, kai, culture, family, thrill, rain-safe, golden hour.
Detail PagesMake each place feel editorial: image, reason to go, story player, timing fit, route fit, stay fit.
PlannerMake the map the flagship surface with logistics, weather, cost, and audio chapters layered in.
CommerceAccommodations and activities appear as matched trip choices, not disconnected listings.
BrandKeep the logo, but free the colour system from teal-only so each region and travel mood can have a pulse.
