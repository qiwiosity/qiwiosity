# Huka Falls — headline (22s)

At Huka Falls, the Waikato River goes from a hundred metres wide to fifteen metres wide in a few seconds. Two hundred and twenty cubic metres of water per second are squeezed through a solid rock canyon — enough to fill an Olympic swimming pool every eleven seconds.
