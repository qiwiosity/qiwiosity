# Ocean Beach / Waipuka — headline (20s)

Long sand, hard surf, and almost nobody. Ocean Beach — Waipuka to the Māori community who have lived here all along — is the rawest coastline in Hawke's Bay. In 1838, William Morris built a whaling station here. In 1887, a ship called the *Go Ahead* broke up offshore. The boiler is still on the rocks.
