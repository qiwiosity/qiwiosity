# Lake Taupō — headline (24s)

Lake Taupō fills the caldera of a supervolcano. The big eruption that shaped it happened twenty-five and a half thousand years ago. The most recent — and one of the most violent eruptions on Earth in the last five thousand years — was around the year 232.
