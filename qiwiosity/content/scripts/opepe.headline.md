# Opepe — headline (26s)

You're driving past Opepe. On the 7th of June 1869, nine Bay of Plenty cavalry troopers were killed in a surprise dawn attack here by Te Kooti's forces. One man escaped through the bush barefoot and naked. The graves are still here, signposted off the road.
