# Esk Valley — headline (25s)

At four in the morning on the 14th of February 2023, the Esk River jumped its banks and buried this valley in silt. Homes. Vineyards. Forty years of one family's wine collection. Cyclone Gabrielle changed Esk Valley forever. What you're seeing now is the rebuild.

---

**Voice direction:** quiet, steady. No drama in the voice — the facts carry it. Let "forty years" land.

**Runtime target:** 22–28 seconds.

**Fact-check:**
- Cyclone Gabrielle flooded the Esk Valley in the early hours of 14 Feb 2023. ✅ Multiple sources (RNZ, Wine Spectator, NZ Herald).
- Philip Barber at Petane Wines dug out 12,000 bottles from ~10 ft of silt — that's the "forty years of one family's wine collection" reference. Reframe as "twelve thousand bottles" if you prefer a harder fact.
- Sacred Hill's Dartmoor vineyard (~37 acres / 15 ha) was permanently lost under silt. ✅ Wine Spectator.
- 11 people died NZ-wide in Gabrielle; damage estimates ~NZ$13–14 billion (various estimates). ✅
