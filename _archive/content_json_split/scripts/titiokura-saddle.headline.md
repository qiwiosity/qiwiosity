# Titiokura Saddle — headline (22s)

You're at 736 metres, on top of the Maungaharuru Range. East: the whole of Hawke's Bay down to the Pacific. West: the Kaweka Ranges and the Mohaka River. Before the 1950s engineers rebuilt this road, truckies used to burn out gearboxes on the climb up.
