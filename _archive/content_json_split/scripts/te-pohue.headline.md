# Te Pōhue — headline (22s)

Te Pōhue has had three hotels. The first was built for Cobb & Co coach passengers in 1869. The second burnt down in 1897 when a farmer's burn-off threw sparks onto the shingle roof. The third was destroyed by the 1931 earthquake. The current township is what stayed.

---
