# Waipunga Falls — headline (22s)

Pull into the lookout on your right. Waipunga Falls drops forty metres in three tiers straight off the side of SH5 — one of the few major North Island waterfalls you can see without getting out of the car. The river behind it drains the Kaimanawa Ranges.

---

**Voice direction:** bright, roadside-friendly. Quick nudge — "on your right" — so drivers know to look now.

**Runtime target:** 20–24 seconds.

**Fact-check:**
- Waipunga Falls ~40 m, three tiers. ✅ DOC / multiple tourism sources.
- Visible directly from SH5 lookout between Te Pōhue and Taupō. ✅
- Waipunga River drains Kaimanawa Ranges before joining Mohaka. ✅
